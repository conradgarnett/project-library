INTERPRETATION
  Using multiple sources DOES widen the best available loop (compare the
  multi-venue vs single-venue net above) and often turns up a positive
  GROSS edge. But each leg pays a taker fee, so ~3 legs cost ~1-2% round
  trip — and cross-venue loops also require moving assets between
  exchanges, which is slow: by the time coins settle, the prices have
  moved. So these are MEASURED opportunities, not executable free money
  unless you pre-position inventory on every venue and act in milliseconds.
