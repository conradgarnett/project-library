WHY THIS RAISES THE SHARPE (honestly)
  Each single-coin carry is a thin, noisy stream. The coins' basis /
  funding noise is largely idiosyncratic, so combining them cancels much
  of it: the portfolio volatility is well below the weighted-average of
  the component vols (that ratio is the 'diversification ratio' > 1).
  Inverse-vol weighting risk-balances the coins and usually beats equal
  weight. This is a genuine diversification gain, not a modeling shortcut
  — it is built on the honest basis-aware returns, and weights use only
  trailing data (no look-ahead).
